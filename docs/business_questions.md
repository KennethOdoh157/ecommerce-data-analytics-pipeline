# Business Questions

This document outlines the key business questions addressed by the analytics pipeline and Power BI dashboards. These questions guide the data modeling decisions and define the analytical value delivered by the project.

---

## Executive Overview

1. What is the total revenue generated over time?
2. How many orders have been placed, and how are they trending?
3. What is the average order value (AOV)?
4. How does sales performance vary by month and year?

---

## Sales Performance

5. Which product categories generate the highest revenue?
6. Which products contribute the most to total sales?
7. How do order items and quantities impact overall revenue?
8. What are the peak sales periods during the year?

---

## Customer Analytics

9. How many unique customers have placed orders?
10. What percentage of customers are one-time buyers versus repeat buyers?
11. How many orders does a repeat customer place on average?
12. How does customer purchasing behavior change over time?

---

## Payment Analysis

13. What payment methods are most commonly used?
14. How does revenue distribution vary by payment method?
15. What is the typical number of installments per order?
16. Do installment-based payments affect order value?

---

## Geographic Insights

17. Which states generate the highest revenue?
18. Which cities have the most customers and orders?
19. How does sales performance vary across regions?
20. Are there geographic patterns in payment preferences?

---

## Order & Delivery Behavior

21. What is the distribution of order statuses (delivered, canceled, etc.)?
22. How long does order fulfillment take on average?
23. Are delivery times correlated with customer reviews?

---

## Customer Feedback

24. What is the overall customer review score?
25. How do review scores vary by product category?
26. Do delayed deliveries impact customer satisfaction?

---

## Strategic Insights

27. Which factors most strongly influence repeat purchases?
28. What opportunities exist to improve customer retention?
29. Which regions or categories present the greatest growth potential?
30. How can payment and delivery insights inform operational improvements?

---

## Summary

These business questions form the analytical foundation of the project. The Bronze–Silver–Gold architecture and dimensional data model were designed specifically to ensure these questions can be answered accurately, efficiently, and at scale using SQL and Power BI.
